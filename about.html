About Alice

My name is Alice, and as far back as I can remember I have been a sufferer of sleep disorder and anxiety. As a child at night I would travel to a far away place where anything was possible. I called this place “Wonderland”. Here I encountered various stressful and also enlightening situations which would always begin with funny as it may sound chasing a rabbit! I would then slip down a tunnel into “Wonderland” where I would face my fears head on while meeting exillerating characters each night. It was only in my twenties when I sought help for my issues that I realised that this was my anxiety manifesting itself. Through various processes and experiences, I have now learned to control this anxiety. I have found happiness in pursuing my passion which is giving others the gift of freedom and happiness. Through the creation of my immersive wellness retreat experience my team are able to touch and change the lives of so many other sufferers.

About Wonderland

Wonderland is an exhilerating wellness centre located in the tranquil setting of the Wicklow mountains. With a wide variety of exhilerating retreats on offer it allows visitors to escape the daily grind and anxieties of modern life to drink in nature and hit the reset button. At Wonderland we embrace the fullness of life and concentrate on tackling mental health issues. We promise you will leave feeling refreshed, balanced and with a new toolkit for dealing with everything life has to throw at you. With our retreats encompassing dream interpretation, anxiety coping skills classes, yoga, meditation and mindfulness there is something for everyone at Wonderland. With our beautiful Wicklow mountain location you can go for a hike on one of our walking trails, relax in a hammock, unwind in our outdoor hottub or just simply be!

Refuel at Wonderland

Did you know that stroking a cat actually relieves stress and anxiety? Well look no further than Wonderland’s very own “Cheshire Cat Cafe” to test out this stress busting theory while ennjoying a full range of delicious organic and healthy meals and snacks. The cafe is open from 7am until 10.30pm, nobody goes hungry in Wonderland. Don’t worry we also have a cat free zone for those of you who aren’t so fond of our feline friends!
