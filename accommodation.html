Glamping Option

This option is perfect for those of you seeking a little advenure and to be surrounded by the sounds and smells of nature without compromising on home comforts. Wonderland offers a range of colourful bell tents complete luxurious bed linend, heating and electricity. After a tranquil night’s sleep enjoy a steaming rainforest shower before setting yourself up for the day’s activities in our onsite Cheshire Cat Cafe.

Book Now from €69 per person

Ecolodge Option

For those who prefer a more solid roof over their heads try one of Wonderland’s luxury ecolodges. Solar powered and constructed entirely from renewable materials, leave your environmental footprint worries behind as you enjoy the best night’s sleep you’ve ever had. Make breakfast from the comfort of your own private kitchen or enjoy a nutritious breakfast served in the Cheshire Cat Cafe.

Book Now from €99 per person
