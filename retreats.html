OUR RETREATS

Here at Wonderland we offer a range of retreat options to choose from. From dream interpretation to creative therapies, we believe that there is something on offer for everyone here.You can chose from one of the below packages or alternatively just drop us a line to tailor something bespoke for you or your group.

Every retreat begins in the morning with a choice of minfulness or yoga class and ends each evening with a meditation session. This is important to switch the mind completely off from the day’s activities. You really are what you eat and here at Wonderland we believe that a healthy body leads to a healthy mind. That is why our cafe only serves organic and healthy produce. We also encourage our guests to explore the grounds through our walking trails and partake in our many shheduled yoga classes throughout the evenings.

The special packages below run on a either a 5 day basis (Monday to Friday) or a shortened 2 day (Saturday and Sunday) version for those who want to try a taster.
